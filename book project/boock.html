A Summary

A pilot crashes in the Sahara Desert and meets a young boy, the Little Prince, from another planet.

As the Prince narrates his journey through various asteroids, he shares insightful observations about life, adults, and the human condition.

Through his friendship with a fox, he learns the profound meaning of love, connection, and responsibility.

"It is only with the heart that one can see rightly; what is essential is invisible to the eye."
